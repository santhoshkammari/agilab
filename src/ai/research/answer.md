## Top Three Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) Papers of the Last Two Months

### 1. **Deploying LLMs With RAG**
- **Source**: [LinkedIn Article](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/top-rag-papers-week-november-3-2024-kalyan-ks-zbkuc)
- **Summary**: This paper presents insights from the development and field-testing of a pilot project that integrates LLMs with RAG for information retrieval. It serves as a good resource by providing best practices and recommendations for adopting RAG systems.
- **Key Contributions**: Best practices for deploying RAG systems, insights from field testing.
- **Paper**: [arXiv:2411.11895](https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.11895)

### 2. **Diverse Multi-Query Rewriting for RAG (DMQR-RAG)**
- **Source**: [LinkedIn Article](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/top-rag-papers-week-november-3-2024-kalyan-ks-zbkuc)
- **Summary**: This paper introduces DMQR-RAG, a Diverse Multi-Query Rewriting framework designed to improve the performance of both document retrieval and final responses in RAG. It also presents an adaptive strategy selection method that minimizes the number of rewrites while optimizing overall performance.
- **Key Contributions**: Framework for query rewriting, adaptive strategy selection.
- **Paper**: [arXiv:2411.13154](https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.13154)

### 3. **Video-RAG**
- **Source**: [LinkedIn Article](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/top-rag-papers-week-november-3-2024-kalyan-ks-zbkuc)
- **Summary**: Video-RAG is a training-free and cost-effective pipeline that employs visually-aligned auxiliary texts to help facilitate cross-modality alignment. It offers significant performance gains across long video understanding benchmarks.
- **Key Contributions**: Training-free pipeline, cross-modality alignment, performance gains on video benchmarks.
- **Paper**: [arXiv:2411.13093](https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.13093)

### Additional Insights from NVIDIA's Blog
- **Source**: [NVIDIA Blog](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/what-is-retrieval-augmented-generation/)
- **Summary**: This blog provides a comprehensive overview of RAG, explaining its importance in enhancing the accuracy and reliability of generative AI models by integrating external data sources. It also discusses the historical context and practical implementations of RAG.
- **Key Contributions**: Historical context, practical implementation examples, and use cases for RAG.

### Conclusion
The recent advancements in RAG, as highlighted in these papers, demonstrate the growing importance of integrating external knowledge sources with LLMs to improve accuracy, reliability, and performance. These developments are poised to have a significant impact on various domains, including healthcare, finance, and customer service, by enabling more trustworthy and efficient AI systems.
